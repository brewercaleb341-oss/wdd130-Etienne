How do I know what assignments are due?

You can check your course syllabus, learning management system (e.g., Canvas), or communicate with your instructors for assignment details and due dates.

Where can I get help if I am struggling?

You can seek help from your instructors, classmates, or the tutoring center on campus.

How do I find a student job?

You can search for student jobs on the university's job board website, at local businesses ect.

When should I start looking for housing?

You should start looking for housing as early as possible, ideally several months before you need to move in.

How can I avoid falling behind?

You can stay organized, keep up with readings and assignments, and communicate with your instructors if you are having difficulty.

How can I make friends?

You can join clubs or organizations, participate in campus events, and engage in conversations with your peers.

New Student Checklist

  • Check Canvas every day.
  • Read your BYU-Idaho email often.
  • Write down assignment due dates.
  • Ask questions before assignments are due.
  • Use tutoring and academic resources early.
  • Make a simple budget for rent, food, and school costs.
  • Take breaks and get enough sleep.
